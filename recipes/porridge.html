Rømmegrøt (Norwegian Porridge)

This recipe is a simple mixture of milk, butter, flour, sugar, and salt. Eat this warm with a hefty drizzle of melted butter and a good sprinkle of cinnamon sugar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 qt whole milk
  • 3/4 c all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 c sugar
  • 1/8 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 c salted butter
  • cinnamon sugar

Steps:

  1. In a large saucepan over medium heat, heat milk to 195°F stirring continuously
  2. Whisk together flour, sugar, and salt
  3. Melt butter and stir into flour mixture to form a thick paste
  4. Transfer the paste to the heated milk and stir constantly until milk starts to boil
  5. Turn heat to low and let cook for 5 mins, stirring often
  6. Spoon into individual bowls and serve with melted butter and cinnamon sugar
